About Us

Welcome to Sweet Tooth 909, your neighborhood bakery bringing sweetness to every moment. Since opening our doors in Rialto, we've been dedicated to baking the freshest pastries, cakes, and cookies for our amazing community. Whether you're here for a quick treat or placing a custom order, we’re here to deliver smiles with every bite.
